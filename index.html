Setting your fitness goal

Envisioning your ideal physique and how that will allow you to live your desired lifestyle is a great way to get your fitness journey started.
It allows you to not only feel the positive effects this tranformation will have on your mind but also gives you a new self-image that you can begin to engrave into your brain.

Nutritional Plan

After figuring out your fitness goal, you must now do a little bit of math to figure out what your calorie intake should be for the day. These following facotrs will have to be taken into account when deciding your calorie intake.Refer to the below link for an estimate on your calorie intake.

Calorie Intake Calculator

•Wether you're wanting to lose or gain weight.This will decide wether you will be in a calorie surplus or deficit.

•Your current weight and daily exercise. Depedning on your current weight and sex you will need to find your maintenance calories.After that you will either add or subtract 100-200 calories to slowly gain or lose weight.

•Tracking your weight and calorie intake daily will allow you to see if you need to increase or decrease your calorie intake. Consuming around a gallon of water a day will also be a great addition to your nutritional plan.
